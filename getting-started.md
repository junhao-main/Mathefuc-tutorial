# Getting Started

Welcome to **Math Proof Practice**. This wiki-style tutorial will help you get the most out of the application.

## What is Math Proof Practice?

A platform for practicing mathematical proofs, exploring definitions and theorems, and organizing your learning. Key features include:

- **Problem Sets** — Collections of proof exercises to practice
- **Library** — Organize definitions, theorems, and propositions
- **Graph Atlas** — Visualize relationships between math objects
- **Projects** — Collaborate with others on shared projects

## Quick Start

1. **Register** an account to save your progress
2. Browse **Problem Sets** to find exercises
3. Use the **IDE** to write and submit proofs
4. Build your **Library** with definitions and theorems you've learned
